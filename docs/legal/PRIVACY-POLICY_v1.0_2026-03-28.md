## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

### 5.1 Retention Periods

We retain personal data for the following periods:

| Data Category | Retention Period | Rationale |
|--------------|------------------|-----------|
| Account Information | Duration of subscription + 7 years | Contract and tax requirements |
| User Activity Logs | 2 years | Security and compliance |
| Voter Data | As directed by Campaign Controller | Controller determines retention |
| Transaction Records | 7 years | Legal and tax compliance |
| Audit Logs | 7 years | Regulatory requirement |
| Support Tickets | 3 years | Quality assurance |
| Deleted Data | 30 days (then permanent deletion) | Recovery window |

### 5.2 Data Deletion Procedures

**Upon Account Closure:**
1. Active data marked for deletion
2. 30-day grace period for recovery
3. Permanent deletion from production systems
4. Backup data purged per retention schedule
5. Deletion confirmation provided

**Campaign-Initiated Deletion:**
- Campaigns may request deletion of their voter data
- Request processed within 30 days
- Export option provided before deletion

**Individual Rights Requests:**
- Data subjects may request deletion
- Verified within 30 days
- Exceptions apply for legal obligations

### 5.3 Data Archiving

Inactive data may be archived:
- Encrypted and separated from active systems
- Retained only as legally required
- Accessible for legal/regulatory purposes
- Deleted when retention period expires

## 6. Your Data Protection Rights

### 6.1 NDPR Rights (Nigerian Residents)

Under NDPR, you have the right to:

**A. Right to Be Informed**
- Transparent information about data processing
- This Privacy Policy fulfills this requirement

**B. Right to Access**
- Request copies of your personal data
- Receive information about processing
- Obtain data portability (machine-readable format)

**C. Right to Rectification**
- Correct inaccurate data
- Complete incomplete data

**D. Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")**
- Request deletion of your data
- Subject to legal retention requirements

**E. Right to Restrict Processing**
- Limit how we use your data
- Maintain data without active processing

**F. Right to Object**
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Object to direct marketing

**G. Right to Withdraw Consent**
- Withdraw previously given consent
- Does not affect lawful processing before withdrawal

### 6.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

EU data subjects have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right not to be subject to automated decision-making
- Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities
- Rights related to data portability

### 6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights:

**Email:** dpo@uradi360.com
**Subject Line:** "Data Rights Request - [Your Name]"
**Include:**
- Full name and contact information
- Description of requested right
- Verification of identity (ID scan)

**Timeline:**
- Acknowledgment: Within 5 business days
- Response: Within 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests)
- No fee for first request; nominal fee for excessive requests

**Verification:**
We must verify your identity before processing requests to prevent unauthorized access.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 8.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Maintain user sessions
- Remember preferences
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve user experience

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies:**
- Purpose: Core platform functionality
- Examples: Session cookies, authentication tokens
- Consent Required: No (necessary for service)

**Functional Cookies:**
- Purpose: Enhanced functionality and personalization
- Examples: Language preferences, display settings
- Consent Required: Yes

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Purpose: Understand usage and improve service
- Examples: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- Consent Required: Yes

**Marketing Cookies:**
- Purpose: Deliver relevant content
- Examples: Advertisement tracking
- Consent Required: Yes

### 8.3 Cookie Management

You can manage cookies through:
- Browser settings (block all or specific cookies)
- Our cookie consent banner
- Third-party opt-out tools

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may prevent Platform functionality.

### 8.4 Analytics and Tracking

We use analytics tools to:
- Understand user behavior
- Identify popular features
- Improve user experience
- Measure campaign effectiveness

Data collected is:
- Aggregated and anonymized where possible
- Subject to contractual data protection terms
