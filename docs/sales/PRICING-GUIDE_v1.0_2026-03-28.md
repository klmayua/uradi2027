## 9. Negotiation Guidelines

### 9.1 Negotiation Strategy

**Never Discount:**
- Without getting something in return
- Without approval (above your authority)
- Just to close a deal
- Because prospect asks

**Always Trade:**
- Discount for longer commitment
- Discount for full prepayment
- Discount for case study participation
- Discount for referral commitment

### 9.2 Concessions Playbook

| If They Ask For... | You Offer... | Value Exchange |
|-------------------|--------------|----------------|
| 20% discount | 15% discount + free training | Higher engagement |
| Extended payment terms | Net 45 (vs. 50/50) | Cash flow trade |
| Additional users | 10 extra users at 50% off | Volume |
| Custom features | Roadmap prioritization | Strategic alignment |
| Lower tier price | Starter tier with Professional features | Trial upgrade path |

### 9.3 Walking Away

**Walk Away When:**
- Prospect demands >30% discount without justification
- Payment terms exceed Net 60
- Prospect won't commit to minimum 6 months
- Prospect is disrespectful or unethical
- Deal is unprofitable even with maximum discount

**How to Walk Away:**
- "I understand your budget constraints. Let's revisit when timing is better."
- "I don't think we're the right fit for your current needs."
- "Perhaps we can help in a future election cycle."
